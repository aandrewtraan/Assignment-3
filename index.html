Overview

Topic

For our project we have decided as a group that we are gonna be creating a food app that allow people to locate restaurants around the area. It differs from other apps such as Uber Eats because it is not a delivery service, but a service to find good food, whether you’re in a new part of town or if you just happen to be an indecisive person. Based on preferences from the user, the app will then choose a restaurant nearby that matches those preferences in order to recommend a place for them to eat.

Motivation

Motivation for this project stems from the annoying indecisiveness that is rather common among people when choosing a place to eat. Either no one unanimously agrees on a restaurant, or no one can think of any particular restaurant. An app that can choose a place for you can significantly cut down the time it takes for people to decide.

Landscape

There are similar apps such as 'Yelp', 'OpenTable', and 'Tripadvisor'. These are the most popular apps for locating restaurants. They provide the ability to search for nearby restaurants under filters that the user can add. Users can also directly schedule bookings through the app. However, none of these apps provide the ability to directly provide restaurant recommendations to users based on their preferences. This does not solve our problem where one would struggle to pick a certain restaurant in the first place.

Scope and Limits

Our project Idea known as “Yumi” is a non-profit app with the essential utilisation of the already well developed google map to allow users to find unique/hidden gems around their area that they may have interest in visiting.

We intend to develop a usable interface application that can successfully show users their location by using the GPS software already installed into their devices, which will allow our application to locate restaurants within their area that matches their preferences. We plan to use an A.I that will be able to pick out restaurants that correlate with the user's liking through machine learning/deep learning.

Scope:

“Yumi” will not be an application that will include other spots other than restaurants that we have determined to match with the preference of the user when they created their account , this is to prevent people using our application in any malicious way to ruin the experience for other users.

Google map is the main open map application that is used within “Yumi” to allow the A.I system to navigate the user to their desired restaurant, before wishing them a good meal through a notification before exiting the google map application.

Our application “Yumi” will have the function of showing user different and unique restaurant through the use of the location that the user must enable within their setting of their electronic devices. which will enable our application to show them the restaurant with the best rated review within their area up till 10 km of where they are currently located, the restaurant will also include photos as well as past customer review about their experience with the restaurant base on our rating system of experience,service,quality and cost.

When the app notices the user leaving the restaurant, it will send a notification to their phone, surveying them on how they enjoyed the food. It will simply be a ranking out of ten. If the user doesn’t open the notification, the app would simply ask them again the very next time they open the app. If the user chooses to complete the survey, the app will store this information and use it in the future.

The app will become more accurate the more information the user provides. For more casual and secretive users, the app will try to get them to turn on their locations or tell them their favourite restaurants by sending them a notification every once in a while, telling them that their experience with the app will become enhanced if they do. We will be careful not to over-step the legal boundaries regarding information/privacy and will hold a very high ethical standard with this app, taking the privacy of the user very seriously. We will take great care in ensuring that the process is as quick and easy as possible by having an easy to manage U.I.

Limit:

Some of the limits/obstacles that we might face during the development of our application would be the development of the A.I that we are going to be using for the application. This is because most of us do not have a lot of experience in the field of A.I, thus creating one would be the biggest issue for our team.

Another obstacle/limit might be the lack of time available to us for the ideas that we want to integrate into our application, which may take a lot more time then the time that has been allocated to complete the project,which may result in someone aspect of the application not being detail as we would like appearing in our final application of “Yumi”.
