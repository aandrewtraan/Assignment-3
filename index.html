Overview

Topic

For our project we have decided as a group that we are gonna be creating a food app that allow people to locate restaurants around the area. It differs from other apps such as Uber Eats because it is not a delivery service, but a service to find good food, whether you’re in a new part of town or if you just happen to be an indecisive person. Based on preferences from the user, the app will then choose a restaurant nearby that matches those preferences in order to recommend a place for them to eat.

Motivation

Motivation for this project stems from the annoying indecisiveness that is rather common among people when choosing a place to eat. Either no one unanimously agrees on a restaurant, or no one can think of any particular restaurant. An app that can choose a place for you can significantly cut down the time it takes for people to decide.
